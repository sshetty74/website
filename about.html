BUDT748 INDUSTRY PRACTICUM
BUDT748 is a capstone course for MS Information Systems (IS) Majors, where students apply their knowledge to develop real-world IS solutions for business clients. Through a complete IS development process, students design and implement functional prototypes that solve practical business challenges, preparing them for careers in technology and consulting.

Professor: Paul T. Shapiro
